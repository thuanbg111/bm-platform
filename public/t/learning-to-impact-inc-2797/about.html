AboutLEARNING TO IMPACT, INC.

LEARNING TO IMPACT, INC. is a U.S. 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 923642797) formed to bring practical support to people in transition. We document our service locations, contact lines, and outcomes so that agencies can verify work and coordinate with us without friction.
