Governance and oversight

Directors of 9 FIRST AVENUE (HOVE) FREEHOLD LIMITED are responsible for the management of the company and for ensuring that statutory and contractual obligations are met.

  • Maintaining statutory registers of directors, shareholdings and key company information.
  • Submitting confirmation statements and other required filings to Companies House.
  • Retaining records of contracts, invoices, correspondence and reports.
  • Ensuring that services provided are in line with written agreements.
  • Using the official company email and telephone for external communication.

Oversight in practice

A simple outline of the governance cycle used for typical projects.

  1. Planning and approval
    Proposed work is reviewed, including scope, costs and any associated risks.
  2. Agreement signed
    An agreement is signed on behalf of the company, confirming responsibilities and deliverables.
  3. Monitoring of delivery
    Progress is monitored and any significant changes are discussed with the client.
  4. Reporting & record keeping
    Work completed is summarised, and records are retained for future reference.
