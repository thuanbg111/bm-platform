About OFF THE BLOCK FOUNDATION

OFF THE BLOCK FOUNDATION is a charitable organization recognized as a 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 921113850). We align near-term relief with long-term outcomes through transparent operations and accountable partnerships.

Relief

Immediate support that reduces harm and connects people to services.

Income

Training and employer links that lead to stable wages and benefits.

Housing

Activation of land to create safe interim sites and paths to permanence.
